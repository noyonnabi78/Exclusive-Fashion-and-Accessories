ELASTICS AND JACQUARD

Elastic: Premium Quality: High-quality elastic is typically made from materials like spandex, elastane, or a combination of rubber and polyester. It has excellent stretch and recovery properties, allowing it to return to its original shape after being stretched. Premium elastic often has a soft finish, good durability, and superior elasticity.

Jacquard Elastic: Premium Quality: Jacquard elastic is a type of woven elastic that incorporates intricate patterns directly into the weave of the fabric. It is made using a Jacquard weaving technique, which allows for detailed and customized designs. These elastics are typically softer and more decorative than regular elastic, often made with blends of cotton, polyester, or spandex.

Lower Price: Elastic can be relatively affordable, especially when made from synthetic materials like polyester or rubber. The price can vary based on the material blend, the width, and the intended use While Jacquard elastic can be slightly more expensive than regular elastic due to the additional weaving process, it is still affordable for most common uses, especially when made from polyester or nylon blends.
