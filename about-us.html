ABOUT US

EXCLUSIVE FASHION AND ACCESSORIES LTD

Exclusive Fashion and Accessoires Ltd is a raising manufacturer company and supplier of high-quality accessories for the apparel, fashion, and retail industries. With years of expertise in the field, we specialize in providing a wide range of products, including woven labels, printed labels, twill tapes, poly bags, drawstrings, gum tapes, elastic, sewing threads, belts, buttons, and more. We are dedicated to offering innovative solutions to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

OUR VISION

To become the preferred global partner for businesses in the garment and retail sector, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

OUR PRODUCTS

We manufacture a comprehensive range of accessories that cater to different segments of the fashion and apparel industry. Our product lineup includes:
1. Woven Labels : Custom-designed woven labels, offering superior durability and intricate detailing for branding and identification.
2. Printed Labels : High-quality printed labels in various colors and designs, ideal for adding logos, care instructions, and other key information.
3. Twill Tapes : Durable, flexible twill tapes for applications in fashion and industrial sectors.
4. Poly Bags : Clear, lightweight, and durable poly bags for packaging, storage, and transportation.
5. Drawstrings : High-strength, adjustable drawstrings for use in apparel, bags, and various products.
6. Gum Tape : Strong, adhesive gum tape for packaging, providing excellent sealing properties.
7. Elastic : A range of elastic products for garments, sportswear, and accessories, offering flexibility and comfort.
8. Sewing Thread : Premium quality sewing threads for various fabric types and applications.
9. Belts : A wide selection of belts, including leather, fabric, and synthetic materials, in different colors and designs.
10. Plastic Buttons : Durable and stylish plastic buttons for garments and accessories.
11. Horn Buttons : High-quality natural horn buttons, crafted for premium fashion pieces.
12. Shank Buttons : Elegant and sturdy shank buttons, ideal for formal wear and high-end garments.
